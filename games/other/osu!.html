osu!


osu! is a very populair rythm game made by peppy. it started first as an individual project.
but since it grew alot it has been grown out to have multiple people work for the project.
osu! also has 4 different game modes. there is: standard, mania, catch and taiko.

osu!standard


osu!standard is one of the populair gamemodes that osu provides.
the standard gamemode requires you to click clircles to the beat of the song.
what people also call the standard game mode is an aim trainer.

osu!mania


osu!mania is a gamemode where you press keys to the beat of the song.
what is important for mania is your accuracy and the patterns.
patterns are a group of notes put into a certain section
some of the known patterns are stairs/streams and jacks.
stairs/streams are a countinouse section where there are 4 notes in a row with a bit of delay.
so you dont hit the 4 notes at once but one to one. those are stairs.
streams are the same as stairs but it just goes back and forward.
jacks are mainly 2 notes you have to hit together in a countinues moition.

osu!catch


osu!catch is a gamemode where you have to catch fruits that are falling from the top of the screen.
there are some fruits that are red that makes you move very fast.

osu!taiko


osu!taiko is a clone of the populair game taiko no tatsujin made by bandai namco.
in this gamemode you have to hit the keys to the beat of the song.
there are 2 different types of notes. youve got a red and blue drum.
there are 2 buttons you can press for each drum to alternate.

osu!lazer


osu!lazer is a gamemode that is still in development.
most of the stuff thats in testing and reworks are made in lazer.
people can also make custom gamemodes in lazer so has there been alot of copies of populair rythm games.
but also there has been some original gamemodes made.
