since its clones/emulators arent really a thing anymore or are privately invited.

the other option is downloading an emulator/clone. a couple emulators/clone is saeurchin or susplayer.

having a desk on a height 70cm or taller.
a 120hz monitor, or 60hz when needed.
and an controller obviously.
