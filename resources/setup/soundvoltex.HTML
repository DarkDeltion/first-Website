sound voltex

there are multiple ways to play sound voltex.
you can unnamed sdvx clone or sdvx konasute.
you can also play sound voltex with a controller or keyboard.
it depends purely on whats your preferd play style.


for an acurate setup you can do 2 things to make your gameplay more enjoyable.
first things:
desk height.
having a perfect desk height doesnt throw yourself off with how you play in the arcade.
you can still just play normally sitting down but its less comfortable when having an arcade size controller.
so for the recommended height it is around 87cm to 92 cm.
second thing is monitor height and recommendations.
for monitors you should always invest in a monitor that has a build in stand that can rotate to 90 degrees.
if your monitor doesnt have it maybe invest in a monitor arm!
you can always switch from 90 degrees to horizontal.
whats also important is that the monitor as atleast 120hz.
you can always play with 60hz but it will be less enjoyable.
